The Enneagram 1 wing 9 type will have to learn how to flow with life by channeling their true feelings and facing their vulnerabilities. This sensitivity will make them wiser and more relaxed with themselves. The world needs the idealist to be ok with its own self so the fire will light the beginning of new times.

Enneagram 1w9 Personality Traits:

Basic Fear

Between 9’s fear of loss and separation and 1’s fear of being corrupt or defective, it’s easy to understand why Enneagram type 1 wing 9‘s main fear is to feel trapped in an unjust and unequal world that’s not worthy of them. Or worse, they fear this world might be a reflection of their inner world. They strive to be better and, in that constant pursuit, they don’t want to lose sight of what’s important: their beloved ones.

The idealist frequently withdraws into their inner self to avoid exposing their traps. They accept their own inconsistencies, so they often bark at their own shadows.

Basic Desire

Enneagram type 1 wing 9‘s most basic desire is to reach the highest version of things.

As they can easily discern right from wrong, they want other people to see things the same way they see them. Enneagram 1 wing 9 types typically shine brighter in calm environments where people are open to hear and share. Under the right conditions, they become truly inspirational and motivational people.

Otherwise, when surrounded by conflict, they will withdraw and avoid any negative feelings. As they have a hard time managing these situations, they can’t really enjoy them and set boundaries without feeling confused.
