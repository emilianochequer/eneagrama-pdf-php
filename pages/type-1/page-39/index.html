Enneagram 1w9: MOTIVATIONS
Enneagram type 1 wing 9 is a very sensitive kind that can easily catch all the negativity and conflicts to better avoid them. If you want to motivate an Idealist, you need to identify what worries them and makes them feel unpleasant. Having their feelings validated is very important for them to be healthy and unfold their brightness.
Enneagram 1w9: JOBS
They want to devote their life to something meaningful. Whatever they do, they give themselves out to make positive and structural changes. Enneagram type 1 wing 9s will use discernment to make big decisions at work. If they feel supported, they freshen up the room. Some careers they may follow are Judge, Social Worker, Environmentalist, Guidance Counselor, Journalist, Public Relations Specialist, Consultant, and Ambassador.
Enneagram 1w9: STRESS
Under stress the Enneagram type 1 wing 9 can be insecure. If they feel they are not making a difference, or if people are constantly correcting them and pointing out their faults, they can be too tough on themselves. This can become a vicious cycle. Recognition and organization will tame their spirit.
