Healthy Enneagram Type 1
How do Type Ones behave when they are in an Healthy state?
At a healthy state, The Reformer feels calm, pleased with themselves and their environment. They achieve an emotional independence, free of external acceptance or third party approval because Type Ones are aligned with their own very intimate purpose. It’s ok to be them. They are on the “correct” path.

Constantly learning and becoming more comprehensive with their own pace makes it easier for Type Ones to understand other’s rhythm too.

They feel inspired by their own dreams. Doing what they have to do in order to achieve bigger goals is what makes Enneagram 1s feel comfortable and vibrant.

The Reformer Type are now wise, keeping both feet on the ground and their heart up in the sky. They know and accept reality, and they don’t fight it out anymore because they’ve discovered that there is where the power of transformation lies.

As Eckhart Tolle says:“Whatever you fight, you strengthen, and what you resist, persists.”

Enneagram 1w2: The Lawyer
Enneagram 1w2 Strenghts
The enneagram Type 1 wing 2 Strengths are related to their capacity to discern what’s the right thing to do and the accurate interpersonal skill to transmit it to other people. They treasure a determinant energy to face reality with a high sense of justice and altruism. Empathy is fundamental for them to identify other people’s needs, which they quickly make their own. People love to hear their speeches. Inspirational and clarifying, the enneagram 1W2, is an innate speaker.
As every human, they want to feel loved but for them this is an inflection point. Nothing makes so much sense without being surrounded by loved ones. They want to change the world, of course, but mostly for the people who live in it.
